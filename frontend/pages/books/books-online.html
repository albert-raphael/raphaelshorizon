Calibre-Web Library Guide

1

Browse the Library

Use the search bar or browse categories to find books. Click on any book cover to open it.

  • Search by title, author, or keyword
  • Filter by genre, rating, or language
  • Sort by popularity, date added, or title
2

Reading Features

Once a book is open, use the reading toolbar for enhanced experience:

  • Adjust font size and typeface
  • Toggle day/night reading mode
  • Change line spacing and margins
  • Bookmark important pages
3

Save Progress

Your reading position is automatically saved. Additional features:

  • Automatic progress syncing
  • Manual bookmarks for key pages
  • Reading history tracking
  • Personal reading statistics

Pro Tips

  • Use keyboard shortcuts: Space for next page, Shift+Space for previous
  • Create reading lists for different topics or moods
  • Enable reading timer to track your reading habits
  • Use fullscreen mode for distraction-free reading
